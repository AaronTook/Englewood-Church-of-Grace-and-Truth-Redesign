Rooted In Grace Classes


Are Rooted in Grace Classes for me?

    Rooted in Grace is a four night class for those who are new to the church, for individuals who want to know more about our church, and for those who are seeking membership.

    The purpose of this class is to give individuals the opportunity to become familiar with Grace and Truthâ€™s church government, the importance of church membership, and distinct doctrines that we hold to. This class is also a requirement for those seeking church membership.

The goal of ths class is for men and women to become rooted so they can grow in the grace of God and the glories of the gospel. The purpose of the church is to edify the saints and equip them for service. (Eph. 4: 12) Our goal is to see that realized in the lives of everyone who is a part of Grace and Truth.

Individuals are expected to listen and ask whatever questions they may have. You will also be required to read Church Membership by Jonathan Leeman. Books will be given to those participating in the class ahead of time so please read chapters 1 and 2 before the first class.

If you attend the class, you will receive a copy of the Statement of Faith, the church's by-laws, and a book called Church Membership by Jonathan Leeman.

This class is generally offered in the Spring and Fall. Classes are held at the church on Monday nights starting at 7:00 P.M. and last for approximately one hour each week.

If you are interested in participating in a Rooted In Grace Class, please email brandon@englewoodgrace.com or contact us directly!
