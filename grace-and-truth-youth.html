Our goal is to see the truths of Scripture taught in the lives of students, and the gospel clearly communicated. We desire a growth in spiritual maturity so that even from a young age they might set an example of what it looks like to live a Christ exalting life. The desire of each class is to magnify Christ through faithful Biblical teaching and loving fellowship. In addition to the in depth studies the teens engage in on Wednesday nights and Sunday mornings, we also have various events throughout the year! Check out upcoming events on the calendar!
